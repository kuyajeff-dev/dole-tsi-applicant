Checklist of Requirement in the Applicant for Electrical Wiring Installation/s of Industrial facilities

In the event that the said Professional Electrical Engineer (PEE) has not yet obtained a Certificate of Appearance, he/she is required to appear personally at the Bureau or at the DOLE Regional / Field Provincial Office so that the corresponding Certificate of Appearance, duly signed by the Bureau Director, the Regional Director, or the Assistant Regional Director (whichever is applicable), will be issued.

For those PEE’s who prefer to apply online, you may send a letter of request to the email address: bwc.secretary@gmail.com

Note:

1) All applications for electrical wiring installations within the area of jurisdiction of the following chartered cities shall be submitted directly to the Building Office of the concerned Local Government Unit (LGU):

1.1 Manila City 1.2 Quezon City 1.3 Caloocan City 1.4 Pasay City 1.5 Batangas City 1.6 Iloilo City 1.7 Bacolod City 1.8 Cebu City 1.9 Mandaue City 1.10 Davao City
